Frequently Asked Questions

  • looks_oneHow do I join or create a video call?
    Go to this link and enter a new channel name and share that name with your friends to create a new video call channel.
    If you want to join an existing call, just enter the channel name created by the host on the same link.
  • looks_twoHow do I create a new broadcast?
    Go to this link and enter a new channel name and share that name with your colleagues or followers to create a new broadcast (or livestream) channel.
  • looks_3My colleague says he's live. Where do I watch his stream?
    Go to this link and enter the channel name created by the host. Click on the Watch Livestream button after this.
    That is it. You can now watch the chosen livestream.
  • looks_4Do speakOut links expire?
    A meeting link remains active until the meeting has ended.
  • looks_5Is speakOut free?
    speakOut is absolutely free for anyone to use.
